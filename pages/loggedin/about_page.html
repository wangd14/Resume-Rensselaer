Our mission is to go beyond merely generating resumes. We aim to build an applicant tracking system that enhances your job prospects. ResumeRensselaer is here to empower RPI students on their journey to success in the professional world.

About Resume Rensselaer

Welcome to ResumeRensselaer, your trusted resume helper designed exclusively for Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) students!

Our platform streamlines the resume creation process by leveraging cutting-edge technology, ensuring that your resume reflects your acievements. Here's a brief overview of what we offer:

    Effortless Resume Generation:

    Simply provide your information into one form, and Resume Rensselaer can generate a resume for you almost instantly.

    Customization Options:

    If any of your information changes, you can simply update your profile with a few clicks.

    Security:

    For added security, we hash our users' passwords prior to storing their login information.

    Functional Requirements:

    Our website ensures selection of desired resume templates, auto-filling of information from profiles, customization options, and the ability to export resumes.

    Performance:

    We prioritize fast response times, ensuring that generating, saving, and loading resumes happen within seconds.
