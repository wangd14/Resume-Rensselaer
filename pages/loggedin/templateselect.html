Template 1

Template 1 is an excellent choice for Computer Science/Information Technology and Web Science Majors. It highlights the importance of projects and experience, putting those at the top of the resume. It also separates hard skills and soft skills within the skills list.

Template 2

Template 2 is a good choice for anybody who lacks project experience. It has several different sections. It shows a user's name and information in the top center. Below that, it displays an objective statement at the top section. Below that is a section for education. Next is work experience, then skills.

Template 3

Template 3 displays a user's name in the top left and information in the top center. After that, it has an objective statement, followed by education and work experience. There's a skills section at the bottom.

Template 4

Template 4 has a user's name and information at the top center. It has an objective statement under the heading "A little about me" and has an education section below that. After education comes work experience, then skills at the bottom.

Template 5

Template 5 displays a user's name and information along the top center, along with a space for a LinkedIn URL. The template then has space for an objective statement, education, work experience, and skills.

Template 6

Template 6 is an excellent choice for an online resume. It has a colored background and displays a user's name, basic information, and picture at the top. It has a couple different sections; with an objective statement and skills on the left, and education and work experience on the right.
